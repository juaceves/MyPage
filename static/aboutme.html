Overview

Hello! My name is Julia Aceves and I am currently a senior at the University of California, San Diego. I am majoring in Cognitive Science with a specialization in Human-Computer Interaction. I enjoy learning and exploring the subjects of design and human learning. It truly fascinates me how, with the proper design, one can create tools that are both user-friendly and powerful beyond imagination. This is why I have chosen to pursue a career in User Interface Design and Web Development. I enjoy working with technology and absoultely find it critical to run all my ideas and creations past the every day user to make sure that they are both innovative and intuitive. I am currently pursuing an internship or entry-level postion in the fields of UX/UI Design, Web Design, or Front-End Web Development. Please see my resume for further details.
